Symptoms for ADHD include

  • Inattentiveness
    • Appear to often make careless mistakes
    • Often doesn’t pay attention to details
    • Problems staying focused on work or activities
    • Doesn’t seem to listen when spoken to
    • Often loses things
    • Forgetful
    • Easily distracted
    • Frequently doesn’t follow through on instructions
    • Not finishing tasks
  • Hyperactivity
    • Fidgety
    • Squirms in chair
    • Impression of restlessness in adolescence (running, unable to stay seated, etc.)
    • Excessive talking
  • Impulsivity
    • Problems waiting their turn
    • Blurts answers before questions are even finished
    • Or blurts out responses in general
    • Interrupts or intrudes in convos or games
